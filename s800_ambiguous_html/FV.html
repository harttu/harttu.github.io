B cells are one of the targets of Friend virus ( FV ) infection , a well - established mouse model often used to study SB__retroviral infections in vivo .
Although B cells may be effective in stimulating cytotoxic T lymphocyte responses , studies involving their role in FV infection have mainly focused on neutralizing antibody production .
Here we show that polyclonal activation of B cells promotes their infection with FV both in vitro and in vivo .
