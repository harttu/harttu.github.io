Sinorhizobium meliloti

Complex regulation of symbiotic functions is coordinated by MucR and quorum sensing in Sinorhizobium meliloti .
In Sinorhizobium meliloti , the production of exopolysaccharides such as succinoglycan and exopolysaccharide II ( EPS II ) enables the bacterium to invade root nodules on SB__Medicago SI__sativa and establish a nitrogen - fixing symbiosis .
