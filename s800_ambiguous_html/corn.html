The phytosanitary importance of the SB__corn leafhopper , Dalbulus maidis ( De Long and Wolcott ) ( Hemiptera : Cicadellidae ) and the planthopper , Delphacodes kuscheli Fennah ( Hemiptera : Delphacidae ) lies in their ability to transmit phloem - associated plant pathogens , mainly viruses and mollicutes , and to cause considerable mechanical damage to corn plants during feeding and oviposition .
